How to Play

Rock, Paper, Scissors: Brawl is a 2D multiplayer game that is a combination of Rock, Paper, Scissors and tag. The game is played in an arena where players can move around and attack other players. Players can choose between three different attacks: rock, paper, and scissors. Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.

You move your character around with the WASD keys. You use Q and E to toggle between rock, paper, and scissors. You attack other players by touching them with your chosen character state. Collect powerups to gain an advantage over your opponents. The game ends when time runs out. The player with the highest kill streak wins.
